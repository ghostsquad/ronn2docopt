Usage:
  naval_fate ship new <name>...
  naval_fate ship <name> move <x> <y> [--speed=<kn>]
  naval_fate ship shoot <x> <y
  naval_fate mine (set|remove) <x> <y> [--moored|--drifting]
  naval_fate -h | --help
  naval_fate --version

Options:
  -h, --help
  -v, --version
  -speed=<kn>
  --pipe
  -r, --roff
  -5, --html
  -f, --fragment
  --manual=<manual>
  --organization=<name>
  --date=<date>
  --style=<module>[,<module>]...
  -w, --warnings
  -W
